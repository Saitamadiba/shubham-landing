This guide will help you understand your lunar placement and begin your journey into the profound wisdom of Jyotish—the "Science of Light."

The Moon in Each Sign

The Moon governs your emotional nature, instinctive reactions, and what makes you feel safe and nurtured.

♈ Moon in Aries

Emotionally courageous and pioneering. Direct, passionate feelings with impulsive reactions. You need independence and action to feel fulfilled. Quick responses that may cool just as fast.

♉ Moon in Taurus

The Moon is exalted here, granting exceptional emotional stability. You find comfort in security, nature, and physical pleasures. Deep loyalty once committed. Patient but sometimes stubborn.

♊ Moon in Gemini

Mind and emotions intertwined—you process feelings through communication. Variety stimulates you; monotony drains. Curious and adaptable, your mood shifts with your environment.

♋ Moon in Cancer

The Moon rules Cancer—its most powerful placement. Profound emotional depth with vivid memory. Home and family are sacred. Remarkable intuition that senses others' feelings.

♌ Moon in Leo

You need to shine emotionally and crave recognition. Generous, warm-hearted love that expects loyalty. Creativity and self-expression are emotional necessities, not luxuries.

♍ Moon in Virgo

You process emotions through analysis, seeking the "why" behind feelings. Service brings satisfaction—you show love through practical help. Order comforts; chaos creates anxiety.

Moon Signs Continued

♎ Moon in Libra

Harmony and partnership are essential to well-being. Natural peacemaker who sees all sides. Beauty soothes your soul. Relationships define your emotional life; you feel complete when sharing.

♏ Moon in Scorpio

Emotions run deeper than most can fathom. Intense passion unafraid of darkness. Trust earned slowly; loyalty absolute once given. Powerful instincts detect dishonesty instantly.

♐ Moon in Sagittarius

Freedom and meaning are your emotional oxygen. Optimism colors your landscape—you believe things will work out. Honest about feelings, sometimes bluntly. Routine suffocates you.

♑ Moon in Capricorn

Emotional maturity came early—old soul energy. Practical processing rarely lets emotion override reason. Achievement and respect matter deeply. Reserved exterior, profound sensitivity within.

♒ Moon in Aquarius

Slightly detached, observational emotional perspective. Feelings connect to humanity—causes move you deeply. Need intellectual freedom; resist possessiveness. Unconventional expression.

♓ Moon in Pisces

Oceanic emotional sensitivity where boundaries blur. Compassion flows naturally; others' pain becomes yours. Creative and spiritual pursuits nourish. Extraordinary intuition, nearly psychic.

The 27 Nakshatras

Each Nakshatra spans 13°20' of the zodiac with its own deity, symbol, and cosmic energy.

1. Ashwini

0°-13°20' Aries • Ketu • Horse's Head

Natural healer with remarkable speed and initiative. Youthful energy throughout life. Innate ability to help others recover.

2. Bharani

13°20'-26°40' Aries • Venus • Yoni

Understanding of life's cycles: creation, preservation, transformation. Powerful creative and sensual energy.

3. Krittika

26°40' Aries - 10° Taurus • Sun • Flame

Sharp, penetrating nature cutting through illusion. Like fire: can illuminate or burn. Fiercely protective.

4. Rohini

10°-23°20' Taurus • Moon • Ox Cart

Embodies growth, fertility, abundance. Magnetic, attractive presence. Appreciates beauty and comfort.

5. Mrigashira

23°20' Taurus - 6°40' Gemini • Mars • Deer

Eternal seeker always searching. Curiosity drives you to collect experiences. Gentleness with alertness.

6. Ardra

6°40'-20° Gemini • Rahu • Teardrop

Known storms that transformed you. Intensity, passion, intellectual brilliance. From sorrow emerges wisdom.

7. Punarvasu

20° Gemini - 3°20' Cancer • Jupiter • Bow

Remarkable resilience—ability to return and begin again. Optimism is your gift. Nurturing nature.

8. Pushya ★

3°20'-16°40' Cancer • Saturn • Udder

Most auspicious nakshatra. Natural ability to nurture, teach, support growth. Generosity defines you.

Hypnotic magnetism and psychological insight. Like serpent, can shed skins and transform. Deep wisdom.

10. Magha

Ancestral power with strong lineage connection. Natural authority and regality. Command respect.

11. Purva Phalguni

14. Chitra

Drawn to beauty, design, visual creation. Artistic talent with eye for aesthetics.

15. Swati

Independence and self-reliance define you. Flexible yet rooted. Value freedom above all.

16. Vishakha

Single-minded determination pursuing goals relentlessly. Ambition burns. Victory through perseverance.

17. Anuradha

3°20'-16°40' Scorpio • Saturn • Lotus

Like lotus from muddy waters, bloom despite challenges. Friendship and devotion sacred.

18. Jyeshtha

16°40'-30° Scorpio • Mercury • Amulet

Energy of the chief, the eldest. Protective authority—defend those in your care fiercely.
